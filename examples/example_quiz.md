## MULTIPLE CHOICE (3 Questions)

**1. What is the capital of France?**

A. London  
B. Berlin  
C. Paris  
D. Madrid

**Answer: C**

**Rationale:** Paris is the capital and largest city of France, located on the Seine River.

---

**2. Which of the following are primary colors? (Select all that apply)**

A. Red  
B. Green  
C. Blue  
D. Yellow

**Answer: A, C, D**

**Rationale:** The primary colors in traditional color theory are red, blue, and yellow. Green is a secondary color made by mixing blue and yellow.

---

**3. What year did World War II end?**

A. 1943  
B. 1944  
C. 1945  
D. 1946

**Answer: C**

**Rationale:** World War II ended in 1945 with the surrender of Germany in May and Japan in September.

---

## TRUE / FALSE (2 Questions)

**4. The Earth is flat.**

**Answer: FALSE**

**Rationale:** The Earth is an oblate spheroid, slightly flattened at the poles and bulging at the equator.

---

**5. Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius at sea level.**

**Answer: TRUE**

**Rationale:** At standard atmospheric pressure (sea level), pure water boils at 100 degrees Celsius (212 degrees Fahrenheit).
